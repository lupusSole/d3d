Open Sourcing, Community, Ethical Capitalism

These are the three pillars on which Dare to Dream was built. We believe that when we come together as a community with a common goal is when the greatest accomplishments are achieved Together is when we have the greatest potential as humans to improve not only the quality of our own lives, but of those around us and even the world we live in. When we are able to support and hold one another accountable we are capable of challenging ourselves to become the greates version of ourselves possible. To become the best version of yourself is the ultimate goal of an indviidual, community, business, and even the planet we live in. Our goal is simply to help facilitate this growth from an ethical and communal point of view.
